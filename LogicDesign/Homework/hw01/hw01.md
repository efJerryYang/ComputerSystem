4. 请化简逻辑函数$F=ABC+ABD+C^{'} D^{'}+AB^{'} C+A^{'} CD^{'}+AC^{'} D+ABCD$，写出最简逻辑函数并在logisim中画出对应功能的最简电路
   **答：**
   化简$F=ABC+ABD+C'D'+AB'C+A'CD'+AC'D+ABCD$
   $$
   \begin{aligned}
   F &= AB(C'D')'+C'D'+AB'C+A'CD'+AC'D\\
   &=AB+C'D'+AB'C+A'CD'+AC'D\\
   &=A(B+B'C)+D'(C'+CA')+AC'D\\
   &=A(B+C)+D'(C'+A')+AC'D\\
   &=AB+AC+A'D'+C'(D'+DA)\\
   &=AB+AC+A'D'+C'D'+AC'\\
   &=AB+A+A'D'+C'D'\\
   &=A+(A'+C')D'\\
   &=A+D'
   \end{aligned}
   $$
